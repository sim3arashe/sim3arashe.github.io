“Tendai Chigavazira has been working in the NGO sector for over 14 years; serving Southern Africa, East Africa, West Africa, Asia and Latin America. Her experience lies in monitoring and evaluation, research, facilitation, donor management, knowledge management, proposal writing and contracts management. She possesses a PhD in Public and Development Management from the University of Witwatersrand with specialisation in Climate Change and Gender. Her passion has always been in community development with special interest in uplifting women. Tendai has a solid academic background in Psychology and Sociology. In addition, she holds a postgraduate diploma in Training Management, which is boosted by her certification in Toastmasters as a speaker and a leader.
